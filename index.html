ABOUT THE NETWORK

ARRAY DEPOT connects more than 6,000 storage facilities across Europe into one unified network. The system provides secure storage solutions for private users and businesses, combining local availability with standardized management processes.

DENSITY

Physical items are integrated into a digital logistics ledger. User inventory can be dispatched, consolidated, or routed between any two depots in Europe overnight. Our proprietary routing protocol optimizes truck cargo space, reducing latency and emission footprints.

ROUTING

Our network spans 29 European states. With over 15,000 automated docking lockers and urban depots, we ensure that personal or business assets are always within 5 to 15 minutes of accessibility. We bridge the gap between suburban warehouses and high-density downtown districts.
